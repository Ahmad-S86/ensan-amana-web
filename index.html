About Us

Our Mission

Improving the lives of vulnerable individuals in Lebanon through essential services in education, health, and social aid.

Our Vision

A just and inclusive society where everyone lives with dignity and basix rights are respected.

Our Philosophy

We serve everyone regardless of gender or nationality, with a special focus on refugees and displaced populations, ensuring no one is left behind.

Our Impact & Programs

Delivering comprehensive support where it's needed most.

Health Sector

Through the Mar Elias Specialized Center, we provide:

  • Comprehensive medical services
  • Surgeries & specialized care
  • Medication for chronic diseases at symbolic prices

Education

Empowering the next generation through:

  • School supplies distribution
  • Literacy programs
  • Support for youth at risk of dropping out

Social & Humanitarian Aid

Immediate relief and long-term support:

  • Food assistance & essential supplies
  • Support for impoverished families
  • Vocational training for economic empowerment

Specialized Support

Targeted interventions for specific needs:

  • Women's psychosocial well-being
  • Child protection services
  • Support for people with disabilities
